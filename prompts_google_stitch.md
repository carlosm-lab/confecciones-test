# Prompts de Diseño para Google Stitch — Sección Corporativa /empresa

Estos prompts están optimizados para **Google Stitch** (`labs.google/stitch`). Proporcionan a la IA la información histórica real y las directrices visuales necesarias para generar diseños profesionales y personalizados, dejando libertad estética estructurada para evitar diseños genéricos.

---

## 🎨 Parámetros Globales de Marca (Incluir en cada Prompt)

- **Colores Oficiales:**
  - Primario (Navy): `#143067`
  - Acento (Rojo Terracota): `#b43024`
  - Fondos / Canvas: `#f8f9fb`
  - Superficies / Cards: `#ffffff`
  - Texto Principal: `#191c1e`
  - Texto Secundario: `#444650`
  - Contenedores Claros: `#d7dffc`
- **Tipografías:** `Noto Serif` para titulares y elementos editoriales, `Manrope` para el cuerpo de texto y elementos de navegación.
- **Atmósfera General:** Moderno, cálido y artesanal. Debe evocar tanto la precisión de un taller de confección como la confianza familiar de una marca con más de 20 años de historia.

---

### 1️⃣ Prompt para la Página Principal Hub (`/empresa`)

```markdown
Design a professional corporate Hub page ("/empresa") for Confecciones Liss, a family-owned custom tailoring and garment manufacturer in San Miguel, El Salvador. The page acts as the main entry point to all corporate information of the brand.

DIRECTIONS:

- Use the brand colors: Primary Navy (#143067), Terracotta Red (#b43024), Crisp Background (#f8f9fb), and Pure White surfaces.
- Use Noto Serif for headlines and Manrope for body text.
- Atmospheric visual style: Warm, professional, clean layout with high contrast and generous whitespace.

REQUIRED SECTIONS:

- Hero: A welcoming introduction to Confecciones Liss as a business. Highlighting its focus on custom tailoring, medical scrubs, and uniforms since 2005.
- Sub-pages Navigation Grid: Clear visual cards or blocks linking to:
  • /empresa/sobre-nosotros (Nuestra Historia)
  • /empresa/filosofia (Filosofía y Valores)
  • /empresa/equipo (Nuestro Equipo)
  • /empresa/media-kit (Media Kit Oficial)
- Timeline Summary: A brief, vertical or horizontal compact visual timeline showing key milestones:
  • 2005: Founded as a home workshop by Iris Lisseth Villacorta de Molina.
  • 2016: Formalization of the Confecciones Liss brand.
  • 2021: Official relocation to Barrio La Merced, San Miguel.
  • 2026: Complete digital transformation of the workshop.
```

### 2️⃣ Prompt para "Sobre Nosotros" (`/empresa/sobre-nosotros`)

```markdown
Design the "Sobre Nosotros" ("/empresa/sobre-nosotros") page for Confecciones Liss. This page narrates the real history of the workshop, building trust and humanizing the brand.

DIRECTIONS:

- Use the brand colors: Primary Navy (#143067), Terracotta Red (#b43024) for accents, and soft backgrounds.
- Keep layout flexible, balanced, and editorial. Use Noto Serif for headers.

REQUIRED CONTENT (Must use real data):

- Hero: "Quiénes Somos". Highlighting our physical workshop located in Barrio La Merced, San Miguel, El Salvador, and what makes us different from mass-produced imports (100% custom-tailored, local employment).
- Our Story (Timeline or Narrative Sections):
  • 2005: Founder Iris Lisseth Villacorta de Molina starts a tiny home workshop with a single sewing machine.
  • 2008-2012: The workshop grows through local word-of-mouth, securing contract to dress the entire student body of "Centro Escolar Profesor Jorge Salomón Granados" (School Code 12855). The founder works with local maquilas to gain industrial scale and quality control expertise.
  • 2020 (Pandemic): The workshop survives the COVID-19 economic shutdown thanks to government relief and family emergency funds.
  • Jan 20, 2021: Confecciones Liss is officially born! Renting a small locale with only $2,000 of emergency funds, starting with one sewing machine, one embroidery machine, one rana machine, one cutting table, and two rolls of fabric.
  • 2021-2025: Rapid organic growth, hiring local tailoring specialists (Lilian Romero, Nubia Vázquez, Blanca Martínez, Carlos Antonio Molina, René Alfonso Méndez).
  • 2026: Complete digital infrastructure expansion designed by Carlos José Molina Villacorta (IT specialist and IEPROES student).
- Workshop Gallery: A placeholder layout for editorial photos showing the actual machinery, sewing thread, and fabrics in the workshop.
```

### 3️⃣ Prompt para "Filosofía y Valores" (`/empresa/filosofia`)

```markdown
Design the "Filosofía y Valores" ("/empresa/filosofia") page for Confecciones Liss. It targets corporate clients, universities, and schools evaluating us as their institutional provider.

DIRECTIONS:

- Keep design clean, corporate yet warm. Emphasize values using high typographic contrast.
- Colors: Primary Navy (#143067) and Forest Green or Terracotta Red (#b43024) for value highlights.

REQUIRED SECTIONS:

- Misión: "Fabricar uniformes personalizados de alta calidad con precisión, durabilidad y entrega puntual, garantizando prendas cómodas que dignifican el trabajo de los profesionales."
- Visión: "Convertirnos en el fabricante de uniformes líder en El Salvador, reconocidos por nuestra artesanía y compromiso con el cliente."
- Valores Centrales (Present in visual blocks with titles and short descriptions):
  • Calidad sin negociación: We don't cut corners on fabric or stitching.
  • Puntualidad: Delivering uniforms on time for students and medical staff.
  • Trato honesto y cercano: Real people helping real professionals.
  • Precisión artesanal: Tailoring experience combined with industrial speed.
  • Orgullo salvadoreño: Proudly sewn in San Miguel, boosting the local economy.
- What Problem We Solve: Contrast standard sizes vs. our custom-tailoring (12+ body measurements), late deliveries vs. coordinated direct logistics, and cheap fabrics vs. certified materials (Sincatex, Ripstop, premium gabardine).
```
